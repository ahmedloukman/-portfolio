About Me

Hi! I'm Ahmed Farouk a curious man who is also a VR developer. After hustling in many different fields including: Logic design, web development, kotlin android development, and learning to code in different languages(C++, java and assembly). I finally found my passion in VR and game development I Started to learn VR development in 2023 using Unity Engine to develop on Oculus Quest 2. I have worked with many of the Quest2 and Quest3 features from simple VR apps to MR features and hand tracking. Here is my portfolio showing What I have learnt so far.
